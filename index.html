About Us

Nellai Muthu Vilas is a family-run traditional sweet shop in the heart of Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, bringing authentic South Indian confections to sweet lovers since 1972. Our recipes have been passed down through the family, using pure cow ghee, natural jaggery, and zero artificial colours or preservatives — ensuring every bite carries the genuine flavour of tradition.

Famous across Coimbatore for our legendary Tirunelveli Wheat Halwa, Mysore Pak, and an extensive range of Ladoo varieties, we serve families at festivals, weddings, and everyday celebrations alike. Whether you need a single box of Kaju Katli or a bulk corporate order, Nellai Muthu Vilas delivers the same time-honoured quality every single time.
